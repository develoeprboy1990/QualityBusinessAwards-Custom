PRESS RELEASE

Press releases are custom written by our team. We will tailor the press release to your business and category. It will include some background information about your company as well as testimonials from your customers that we will source from review sites such as Google My Business.

Once we have drafted the press release we will email it to you for approval. You’re welcome to make or request the edits that you would like made. We will make as many edits until you’re satisfied with the press release.

Once you have approved the press release for distribution we will then begin distributing the press release to over 300 various news outlets in North America. 3-5 days later we will send you the links of the news outlets that have published the press release.

After you've given the green light, we'll proceed with distributing the press release to a range of news outlets.
